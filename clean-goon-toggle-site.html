Why You Shouldn't Get a Job & Just Goon

A calm, thoughtful look at why stepping off the 9-to-5 treadmill can be a path to freedom and fulfillment.

  • Design Your Own Day: Freedom to decide when to rest, work, or play without clocking in or out.
  • Escape Stress: Avoid toxic workplaces and the pressure of deadlines that drain your mental energy.
  • Follow Your Passions: Time to explore hobbies and skills that bring joy and meaning beyond a paycheck.
  • Build Meaningful Connections: More time for friends, family, and communities you truly care about.
  • Prioritize Self-Care: Less burnout, more sleep, and the space to heal and recharge.
